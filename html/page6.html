I am Tanjiro

🌟 Tanjiro Kamado – The Gentle Warrior Tanjiro is the main hero of Demon Slayer — and honestly, he’s not your typical shonen boy. He’s kind, respectful, super polite… and will still slice a demon’s head clean off if you mess with innocent people or his sister. 😤💢 🔥 His Backstory: Tanjiro was living peacefully with his family, selling charcoal. One day, he returns home to find them slaughtered by a demon, and his sister Nezuko turned into a demon. From that moment, he decides to become a Demon Slayer to protect others and find a way to turn Nezuko human again. 💔🩸 🌊 His Abilities: Water Breathing (early on) — smooth, flowing sword moves like Water Surface Slash 💧. Later, he learns Hinokami Kagura (Sun Breathing) — fiery and powerful, passed down from his father 🔥. Has a super strong sense of smell, which he uses in battle to sniff out the “Opening Thread” — the perfect moment to strike 🎯. 💚 His Personality: Gentle, empathetic, and super respectful — even to enemies 😭. Cries easily, but never gives up. Treats Nezuko like a queen 🐾 and protects her at all costs. Always stands up for what’s right, even when he’s badly hurt. 🥺 Special Moments: When he carries Nezuko on his back through snow… 💔 The epic Hinokami battle against Rui in Season 1 — tears and goosebumps everywhere 💥😭. His sweet moments with Zenitsu and Inosuke — like an exhausted mom with two chaotic kids 🤣💛💙.
